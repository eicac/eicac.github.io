Electron-Ion Collider Accelerator Collaboration will meet on May 20, 2026 at premiere accelerator physics conference - IPAC 2026. 

This special session will bring together the EIC and FCC communities to discuss synergies between the two collider design efforts — from accelerator technologies and machine design challenges to opportunities for collaboration across projects.

As the next generation of collider facilities takes shape, strengthening connections between communities is more important than ever. We look forward to inspiring discussions and new ideas emerging from this exchange. 
The event is open and we particularly encourage young researchers to attend and discover the exciting opportunities.
