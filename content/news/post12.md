Dr.  Wolfram Fischer has been named co-chair of the Electron-Ion Collider Accelerator Collaboration (https://eicac.org/). Fischer will partner with Tatiana Pieloni of École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne to guide more than 20 international institutions as the EIC moves from design to construction.
